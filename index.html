🐾 Sandbox Handbook

Command protocols for the feral felines

🐟 Spawning Treats

Clicking the Feed Fish button drops radioactive fish treats 🐟 that swim globally around your screen. Nearby kittens will immediately alert, pursue, scale panels, and pounce to eat the snack!

🍃 Catnip Overdrive (Hyper Mode)

Triggering Catnip sprays the screen with an emerald radioactive mist and activates a dreamy overdrive state. Kittens rotate with barrel backflips, float lazily, glow neon green, and emit waves of glowing leaves.

🔴 Tactical Laser Scope

Enabling the Laser changes your cursor into a high-tech reticle. Kittens enter hunting mode, scaling any UI element, card, or table vertically and horizontally to chase the targeting dot!

👆 Drag & Drop Physics

Grab and carry any kitten anywhere on your active screen using smooth scrolling dragging logic. On release, gravity pulls them naturally down to stand on the nearest header or metrics card.

💖 Affection & Liking Stats

Feeding fish treats or carrying felines around builds their liking level. Happy kittens emit floating neon hearts ❤️ and Purr periodically.

🐈 Tail Tap Action (Startle)

Tap a kitten near their tail to startle them. They will jump, hiss, and display a warning indicator ❗.
